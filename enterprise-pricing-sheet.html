Enterprise Pricing Guidelines

Omia Studio Custom/Enterprise Plans

1. Enterprise Tier Structure

Note: All pricing is per project, per month. Each project is billed independently.

Tier Price Per Project/Month AI Credits Deployments Key Features
Starter Enterprise $299 - $499 50,000 AI credits/mo Unlimited SSO, Custom branding, Priority email support
Growth Enterprise $799 - $1,499 150,000 AI credits/mo Unlimited Dedicated success manager, SAML, White-label, API access
Premium Enterprise $2,500+ Unlimited AI credits Unlimited 24/7 support, On-premise option, Custom SLA, Compliance

2. Key Pricing Factors

2.1 AI Credit Usage

Pricing is based on AI credit consumption per project:

  • 50,000 AI credits/month: $299-$499 per project
  • 150,000 AI credits/month: $799-$1,499 per project
  • Unlimited AI credits: $2,500+ per project
  • Additional credits (if needed): $5 per 10,000 credits
AI Credit Usage: 1 credit ≈ 1 AI code generation request, 5 credits ≈ 1 wireframe generation, 10 credits ≈ 1 full page generation

2.2 Deployment & Bandwidth Usage

Deployment resources and bandwidth consumption:

  • Standard deployments: Unlimited (included in base)
  • Bandwidth: 100GB/month included per project
  • Additional bandwidth: $0.10 per GB overage
  • Storage: 50GB included per project
  • Additional storage: $0.15 per GB/month

2.3 Support Level Add-ons

Support tiers apply per organization (not per project):

  • Standard support (email, 24-hour response): Included in base
  • Priority support (phone + email, 4-hour response): +$200-$400/mo per organization
  • Premium support (24/7 availability, 1-hour response): +$500-$800/mo per organization
  • Dedicated success manager: +$1,000-$2,000/mo per organization

2.4 Advanced Features Pricing

Advanced features are billed per project unless specified:

  • SSO/SAML integration: +$100-$200/mo (organization-level)
  • White-label deployment: +$300-$500/mo per project
  • API access: +$200-$400/mo per project
  • On-premise deployment: +$2,000-$5,000/mo per project (plus setup fee)
  • Custom integrations: $5,000-$25,000 per integration (one-time)
  • Compliance certifications (SOC2, HIPAA, GDPR): +$500-$1,500/mo (organization-level)

2.5 Third-Party Web Hosting & Domain Control

  • Custom domain connection (basic DNS): Included in all Enterprise tiers
  • Advanced domain management (multi-domain, subdomain routing): +$50-$150/mo
  • Managed web hosting (AWS/GCP/Azure infrastructure): +$200-$800/mo based on traffic
  • CDN & performance optimization: +$100-$300/mo
  • SSL certificate management (custom certificates): +$50-$100/mo
  • Load balancing & auto-scaling: +$300-$600/mo
  • Dedicated IP addresses: +$25 per IP/mo
  • White-label domain hosting (client-facing): +$400-$800/mo
  • Full infrastructure management service: +$1,000-$3,000/mo
Note: Hosting costs are based on estimated monthly traffic. Organizations exceeding 100GB bandwidth/month or 1M requests/month will be quoted custom infrastructure pricing.

2.6 Contract Length Discounts

  • Month-to-month: Full price (no discount)
  • 6-month commitment: 5% discount
  • 12-month commitment: 10-15% discount
  • 24-month commitment: 15-20% discount
  • 36-month commitment: 20-25% discount

3. Sales Negotiation Guidelines

3.1 Discount Authority Levels

  • Sales Representatives: Up to 10% discount (no approval needed)
  • Sales Managers: Up to 20% discount
  • Director of Sales: Up to 30% discount
  • C-Suite Approval Required: 30%+ discount

3.2 Setup & Onboarding Fees

  • Starter Enterprise: $0-$500 one-time (waived for 12-month contracts)
  • Growth Enterprise: $1,000-$2,500 (includes dedicated onboarding sessions)
  • Premium Enterprise: $5,000-$15,000 (includes custom implementation & training)
  • Data migration services: $2,500-$10,000 (project-specific quote)
  • Custom training sessions: $1,500 per day (on-site or remote)
  • Third-party integration setup: $3,000-$8,000 per integration

Typical Needs: Heavy AI usage (150K credits/mo), white-label, custom domain, managed hosting

Total: $1,899/month for 1 project

Scenario D: SaaS Startup with Multiple Product Projects

Typical Needs: 3 product projects, high AI usage, API access, white-label

Total: $2,168/month for 12 projects

5. Special Pricing Programs

5.1 Academic & Non-Profit Discount

Eligibility: Verified educational institutions and registered 501(c)(3) non-profits

  • Discount: 40-50% off standard Enterprise pricing
  • Setup fees: Waived
  • Free training workshops: Included
  • Requirement: Annual billing, proof of status

5.2 Startup Program

Eligibility: Companies less than 3 years old, under $1M annual revenue

  • Discount: 25-35% off for first year
  • Benefit: Growth Enterprise features at Starter Enterprise pricing
  • Free success manager for 6 months
  • Requirement: Equity stake (0.1-0.5%) OR detailed case study with logo usage rights

5.3 Partner/Agency Program

Eligibility: Agencies and consultants bringing multiple client accounts

  • Revenue share: 20% recurring commission on client accounts
  • White-label options: Provided at cost
  • Hosting management: Discounted infrastructure rates
  • Co-marketing opportunities and dedicated partner support
  • Minimum: 5 active client accounts to maintain partner status

5.4 Regional Pricing Adjustments

Applicability: Customers in developing economies (based on World Bank classifications)

  • Discount: 30-40% off standard pricing
  • Payment: Local currency and payment methods required
  • Support: Regional business hours apply
  • Review: Pricing subject to annual GDP/currency review

6.3 Infrastructure Provider Options

Clients can choose their preferred infrastructure provider with managed services:

  • AWS (Amazon Web Services): Most popular, comprehensive services (+$200-$800/mo management fee)
  • Google Cloud Platform (GCP): Excellent for AI/ML workloads (+$200-$800/mo management fee)
  • Microsoft Azure: Best for enterprise integration (+$200-$800/mo management fee)
  • DigitalOcean: Cost-effective for smaller deployments (+$150-$500/mo management fee)
  • On-Premise: Client-hosted infrastructure with remote management (+$2,000-$5,000/mo)
Note: Infrastructure costs (compute, storage, bandwidth) are pass-through charges billed directly by the provider. Management fees cover Omia Studio's configuration, monitoring, security, and optimization services.

7. Payment Terms & Billing

7.1 Standard Payment Terms

  • Payment method: Credit card, ACH transfer, wire transfer, or invoice (NET 30 for Enterprise)
  • Billing cycle: Monthly in advance or annual prepayment
  • Price lock: Guaranteed for contract duration
  • Auto-renewal: Subscriptions renew automatically unless cancelled 30 days before end date
  • Cancellation: No refunds; access continues until end of billing cycle, then projects revert to free tier immediately

7.2 Annual Prepayment Benefits

  • 10-15% discount (in addition to commitment discount)
  • Waived setup fees (up to $2,500 value)
  • Priority onboarding (expedited implementation)
  • Flexible payment schedule available (quarterly installments for Premium tier)

8. Sales Team Contacts

Internal Escalation Path

  • Standard Enterprise Quotes ($299-$1,499/mo): Sales Representatives
  • Premium Quotes ($1,500-$5,000/mo): Sales Managers
  • Strategic Accounts ($5,000+/mo): Director of Sales
  • Custom Integrations & Special Terms: VP of Sales + CTO approval
Important: All pricing guidelines are subject to change. Always verify current rates with the Sales Operations team before finalizing quotes. For deals requiring >30% discount or custom terms outside these guidelines, submit a deal desk request for executive approval.
